01
How do you build a brand that belongs to a place — without becoming a cliché of it?

ecomm Northwest is a community for founders, operators, and marketers driving ecommerce growth across the Pacific Northwest. The challenge wasn't to make it look outdoorsy. It was to make it feel rooted — like it could only have come from here.

03 — The palette
Not chosen.
Excavated.

Every color was pulled from the Pacific Northwest landscape. Soil Maroon from the earth underfoot. Deep Timber from the forest canopy. Lichen Glow from the impossible yellow-green that grows on rocks. These aren't brand colors — they're field notes.

04 — The logo
The linked C and O.
Connection built
into the mark.

The logo isn't a wordmark with a symbol bolted on. The linked C and O are the wordmark — a nod to connection, collaboration, and the shared progress that defines the community. Every spacing rule was built to protect that spark.

Cap height: 40px
Clear space: 0.5x = 20px
Min width: 120px
Grid: 240 × 80px
